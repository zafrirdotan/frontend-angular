About Shopit-GPT

Empowering Creativity and Efficiency with AI

Welcome to Shopit-GPT, an experimental platform designed to unleash the potential of artificial intelligence in everyday tasks and online shopping experiences.

Feature 1: AI-Enhanced Shopping Cart

Shopping online is about to get a lot smarter and more personalized. Our app leverages the OpenAI API to revolutionize how you shop on the internet. Imagine an AI-based cart that not only understands your shopping needs but also makes intelligent suggestions, manages your shopping list, and optimizes your online purchasing process. It's about making shopping seamless, efficient, and tailored to your preferences.

Feature 2: An experimental clone of Chat GPT Interface

This is an experimental clone of Chat GPT Interface. It is a simple chat interface that allows you to chat with an AI assistant. The assistant is powered by OpenAI's GPT-3, which is capable of understanding and responding to natural language input. The interface is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, allowing you to have a natural conversation with the AI assistant. You can ask it questions, get recommendations, or just have a chat. The possibilities are endless!

Built on a Foundation of Modern Technologies

At the core of our app lies a robust technology stack consisting of NestJS, Angular, MongoDB, and deployment on AWS (Amazon Web Services). This powerful combination of modern development frameworks and cloud hosting solutions enables us to provide a high-performance, scalable, and secure platform. Designed to meet the current demands of our users while also anticipating future needs, our app stands at the forefront of the digital landscape, ready to adapt and evolve.
